Footwear

Formal Shoes
  • **Oxfords**: Black or dark brown leather Oxford shoes are the epitome of elegance. Stick to traditional **cap-toe** or **plain-toe** designs for a timeless look.
  • **Loafers**: For a more casual vibe, loafers in leather or suede are perfect. **Penny loafers** or **tassel loafers** in brown or black can elevate a simple outfit.
  • **Brogues and Derbies**: These are versatile, timeless options that can be worn with suits or more casual trousers. **Brown brogues** or Derbies are perfect for semi-formal occasions.
Casual Shoes
  • **Boat Shoes**: For casual summer outings, **leather boat shoes in brown or navy** work perfectly with chinos or tailored shorts.
  • **Driving Shoes**: Soft leather driving shoes in muted tones offer a more relaxed but still luxurious option.
